Laying the Foundation for Lifelong Learning

Adiuvaret Foundation’s Nursery Teacher’s Training Program is designed to prepare educators for the crucial role of shaping young learners during their formative years. This program emphasizes child psychology, early childhood pedagogy, and interactive teaching methods to create engaging and nurturing learning environments.

AREF NTT Programs

Two Comprehensive Programs Available

AREF offers two NTT programs: a 12-month comprehensive program with internship & placement support, and a 6-month program for aspiring teachers. Academic partnerships with GBSRC + Dr. D.Y. Patil Vidyapeeth ensure credibility and recognition.

Partner schools can directly admit students or enroll students who register directly with AREF.
