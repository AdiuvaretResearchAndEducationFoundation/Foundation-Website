Teachers Manual for NTT

Our comprehensive Nursery Teachers Training Program Franchise Program manual is designed to equip educators with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in early childhood education. This resource covers fundamental concepts of child development, age-appropriate teaching methodologies, and effective classroom management techniques for children aged 2-6 years.

The manual includes practical activities, assessment strategies, parent communication guidelines, and safety protocols essential for creating a nurturing learning environment. It serves as an invaluable resource for both new and experienced nursery teachers seeking to enhance their professional practice.

Complete NTT Learning Framework

Our NTT Manual follows a structured approach to nursery teacher training, covering all essential aspects of early childhood education. The curriculum is designed to build competency progressively from basic child psychology to advanced teaching methodologies.

Module 1: Foundation of Early Childhood
Child psychology, development theories, and learning styles
Module 2: Classroom Environment & Management
Creating conducive learning spaces and behavior management

Age-appropriate teaching techniques and curriculum planning
